Bahá’í Feasts & Holy Days

The Bahá’í Faith celebrates a number of special days each year, following the Badí’ calendar. These dates are shown below, displaying the name of each day or month, and when it starts.

Days begin at sunset, so your location is needed so that the system can determine when sunset will be for you.
